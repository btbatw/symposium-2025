Posters and Short Talks

Dear BTBA friends, we warmly invite you to be part of something exciting! Submit your poster abstract for the 2025 BTBA Annual Symposium for a chance to win a $30 gift card and have your registration fee waived. With ready-to-use poster abstract templates, getting started is easy. Don't miss this excellent opportunity to share your amazing work—we can't wait to meet you!

Poster presenters will be eligible for “Best Poster Awards” and “Audience Choice Award”. In addition, 3 posters from the applications will be selected for oral presentations at the symposium. The selected presenters will be awarded a gift card ($30 in value) and their registration fee will be waived.

Please note: due to space limitations, a person can only submit one poster abstract and be the main presenting author on one poster. (You can be co-authors on multiple posters but can only be the main presenter of ONE poster.)
